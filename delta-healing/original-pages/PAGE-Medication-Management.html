What is Psychiatric Medication Management?

Medication management involves prescribing, monitoring, and adjusting psychiatric medications to effectively treat mental health conditions while minimizing side effects. Our providers conduct regular follow-ups to ensure optimal treatment outcomes.

At Delta Healing Solutions, we take a personalized approach to medication management, working closely with you to find the right medication at the right dose.

Our Approach

Collaborative Care

We believe in collaborative care. Your input is essential to finding the right treatment. We'll:

  • Explain how medications work and what to expect
  • Discuss potential side effects and how to manage them
  • Monitor your response and adjust dosing as needed
  • Answer all your questions and address concerns
  • Coordinate with your other healthcare providers

Conditions We Treat

  • Depression - SSRIs, SNRIs, atypical antidepressants
  • Anxiety Disorders - Anti-anxiety medications, beta-blockers
  • ADHD - Stimulants and non-stimulant medications
  • Bipolar Disorder - Mood stabilizers, antipsychotics
  • Insomnia - Sleep aids when appropriate
  • PTSD - Evidence-based medication protocols
  • OCD - SSRIs and other effective treatments
  • Psychotic Disorders - Antipsychotic medications

What to Expect

Initial Visit

  • Comprehensive psychiatric evaluation (if not already completed)
  • Discussion of medication options
  • Prescriptions provided if appropriate
  • Education about your medication
  • Follow-up scheduled

Follow-Up Appointments

  • Initially: Monthly or more frequently to monitor response
  • Once stable: Every 2-3 months for ongoing management
  • Assessment of symptom improvement
  • Monitoring for side effects
  • Medication adjustments as needed
  • Prescription refills

Safety & Monitoring

Your safety is our priority. We carefully monitor:

  • Therapeutic response - Are your symptoms improving?
  • Side effects - Any unwanted effects?
  • Drug interactions - Checking compatibility with other medications
  • Lab work - When necessary for certain medications
  • Blood pressure - For medications that may affect it

Important Safety Tips

  • Never stop psychiatric medications abruptly - Always consult your provider first
  • Take as prescribed - Consistency is key to effectiveness
  • Report side effects - We can often adjust or switch medications
  • Keep all appointments - Regular monitoring ensures safety
  • Ask questions - Understanding your medication helps engagement
