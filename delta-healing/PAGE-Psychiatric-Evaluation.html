Comprehensive Mental Health Assessments in Delta, UT

What is a Psychiatric Evaluation?

A psychiatric evaluation is a comprehensive assessment of your mental health conducted by a qualified psychiatric provider. The evaluation helps diagnose mental health conditions and develop an effective, personalized treatment plan.

At Delta Healing Solutions, our evaluations are thorough, compassionate, and focused on understanding your unique needs.

What to Expect During Your Evaluation

Initial psychiatric evaluations typically take 60-90 minutes. Our provider will conduct a detailed interview covering:

  • Current symptoms and concerns
  • Medical and psychiatric history
  • Family history of mental health conditions
  • Medication history
  • Social and occupational functioning
  • Sleep patterns, appetite, and energy levels
  • Substance use history

Conditions We Diagnose and Treat

  • Depression - Major Depressive Disorder, Persistent Depressive Disorder
  • Anxiety Disorders - Generalized Anxiety, Panic Disorder, Social Anxiety, Phobias
  • ADHD - Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (children and adults)
  • Bipolar Disorder - Bipolar I, Bipolar II, Cyclothymic Disorder
  • PTSD - Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder
  • OCD - Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder
  • Mood Disorders - Various mood-related conditions
  • Psychotic Disorders - Schizophrenia and related conditions

After Your Evaluation

Following your assessment, your provider will:

  • Discuss their diagnostic impressions with you
  • Explain your diagnosis in understandable terms
  • Develop a personalized treatment plan
  • Discuss treatment options (medication, therapy, TMS, Spravato, etc.)
  • Answer all your questions
  • Schedule follow-up appointments as needed

Do I Need a Referral?

Referral requirements depend on your insurance plan. Some require a referral from your primary care physician, while others allow self-referral for mental health services.

What to Bring to Your Appointment

  • Your insurance card(s)
  • Photo ID
  • List of current medications
  • Previous psychiatric records (if available)
  • List of questions or concerns

Confidentiality

All information shared during your evaluation is confidential and protected by HIPAA laws. We will not share your information without your written consent, except as required by law (e.g., imminent danger to self or others).

Insurance & Payment

We accept most major insurance plans, including Medicare and Medicaid. Our staff will verify your benefits prior to your appointment and help you understand your coverage.
