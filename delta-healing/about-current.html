Transforming mental healthcare

Delta Healing Solutions is a non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing mental health care. Mental health challenges impact us all, directly or indirectly, causing profound pain and suffering. Our mission is to heal these wounds, alleviate suffering, and empower individuals to lead purposeful lives.

Our focus is on improving the Quality, Accessibility, and Affordability of mental health services.

  • Quality: Under the guidance of a board-certified psychiatrist, we implement evidence-based practices to provide safe and effective services. Our commitment extends to ongoing research initiatives aimed at enhancing the safety and efficacy of treatment methods.

  • Accessibility: We strive to bridge the gap by offering increased access to specialty care in underserved rural communities, where such resources are often limited.

  • Affordability: As a non-profit, we leverage research and scholarship programs to support under-insured and low-income individuals, easing the financial burden of mental health care for those in need.
