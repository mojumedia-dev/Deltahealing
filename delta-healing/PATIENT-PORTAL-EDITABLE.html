Your Privacy & Security

The patient portal uses industry-standard encryption to keep your health information safe and secure. All communications through the portal are HIPAA-compliant and protected.

Note: The patient portal is for non-urgent matters only. If you have a medical emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. For urgent clinical questions, please call our office directly.
