Who We Are

Delta Healing Solutions is a non-profit mental health clinic serving Delta, Fillmore, Oak City, Hinckley, and all of Millard County, Utah. We are committed to making high-quality psychiatric care accessible to rural communities that often lack mental health resources.

Founded on the belief that everyone deserves compassionate, evidence-based mental health treatment, we combine cutting-edge therapies like NeuroStar TMS and Spravato (esketamine) with traditional psychiatric services to provide comprehensive care for our community.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate, and evidence-based mental health care to underserved rural communities in Utah, reducing barriers to treatment and improving lives through innovative therapies and personalized psychiatric care.

Our Values

Compassion

We treat every patient with empathy, respect, and dignity. Mental health challenges are real, and we're here to support you without judgment.

Accessibility

We accept most insurance plans and work to ensure cost is not a barrier to quality mental health care in rural Utah.

Excellence

Our team is trained in the latest evidence-based treatments and committed to providing the highest standard of psychiatric care.

Innovation

We invest in cutting-edge therapies like TMS and Spravato to give our patients access to the most advanced treatment options available.

Community

We're dedicated to serving Millard County and surrounding areas, bringing world-class mental health care close to home.

Hope

We believe recovery is possible. Even when traditional treatments haven't worked, we offer new pathways to healing and wellness.

Thomas Rayner, MD

Clinical Director

Dr. Rayner is an alumnus of the University of Utah School of Medicine and has successfully completed his residency training in adult psychiatry at the University of Nevada, Reno.

A proud member of both the American Psychiatric Association and the clinical TMS society, Dr. Rayner is dedicated to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of a spectrum of mental health conditions. Dr. Rayner also serves as a medical officer in the Utah Army National Guard.

His expertise encompasses mental disorders, emotional disorders, psychotic disorders, mood disorders, anxiety disorders, substance-related disorders, as well as sexual and gender identity disorders, and adjustment disorders. Dr. Rayner's clinical focus revolves around providing comprehensive care to individuals and families grappling with serious mental illness.

Angie Young

Patient & Certification Coordinator | TMS Tech

Mental health has always been close to my heart. Growing up, my family had an open culture around mental health, and I watched my parents work tirelessly to heal from generational trauma. Their journey—and the incredible impact of having the right support—ignited my passion for helping others access the care they need.

At Delta Healing Solutions, I'm often the first point of contact for patients. I manage calls, schedule appointments, and ensure our front office is a welcoming space. I know how important that first interaction can be, and I strive to make it a positive one.
