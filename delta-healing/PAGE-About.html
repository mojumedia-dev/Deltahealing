Who We Are

Delta Healing Solutions is a non-profit mental health clinic serving Delta, Fillmore, Oak City, Hinckley, and all of Millard County, Utah. We are committed to making high-quality psychiatric care accessible to rural communities that often lack mental health resources.

Founded on the belief that everyone deserves compassionate, evidence-based mental health treatment, we combine cutting-edge therapies like NeuroStar TMS and Spravato (esketamine) with traditional psychiatric services to provide comprehensive care for our community.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, compassionate, and evidence-based mental health care to underserved rural communities in Utah, reducing barriers to treatment and improving lives through innovative therapies and personalized psychiatric care.

Why We're Different

As a non-profit organization, our focus is on patient outcomes, not profits. We work with most insurance providers and strive to make mental health care affordable and accessible to all who need it.

We bring advanced treatments to rural Utah that are typically only available in major cities:

  • NeuroStar TMS Therapy - FDA-cleared magnetic brain stimulation for depression
  • Spravato Treatment - Breakthrough esketamine therapy for treatment-resistant depression
  • Comprehensive Psychiatric Services - Evaluations, medication management, and ongoing care
  • ADHD & Cognitive Testing - Professional assessments for children and adults

Serving Rural Utah

Mental health care is often harder to access in rural communities. Long drives, limited providers, and stigma can prevent people from getting the help they need. We're changing that.

Delta Healing Solutions brings specialized psychiatric treatments directly to Millard County, eliminating the need to travel hours to Salt Lake City or other urban centers. We're here, in your community, ready to help.
