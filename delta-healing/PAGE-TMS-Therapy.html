What is TMS Therapy?

TMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) is an FDA-cleared, non-invasive procedure that uses magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in the brain to improve symptoms of depression. TMS is proven effective for patients who haven't responded adequately to antidepressant medications.

Delta Healing Solutions offers NeuroStar TMS, the #1 TMS system with over 6 million treatments performed nationwide.

How TMS Works

During a TMS session, a treatment coil is placed against your head. The coil delivers brief magnetic pulses to stimulate nerve cells in the region of your brain involved in mood control and depression. These pulses help "wake up" underactive areas of the brain, restoring normal function and alleviating depression symptoms.

Who is TMS For?

TMS therapy may be right for you if:

  • You've been diagnosed with major depressive disorder (MDD)
  • You haven't responded adequately to antidepressant medications
  • You can't tolerate the side effects of antidepressants
  • You're looking for a non-medication treatment option
  • Previous treatments haven't provided lasting relief

Side Effects

TMS is well-tolerated with minimal side effects. The most common side effect is mild scalp discomfort during or after treatment, which typically resolves after the first week. Unlike antidepressants, TMS does not cause:

  • Weight gain
  • Sexual dysfunction
  • Nausea or digestive issues
  • Drowsiness or sedation
  • Memory problems
