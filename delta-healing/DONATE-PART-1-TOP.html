Why We Need Your Support

Delta Healing Solutions is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to providing accessible, compassionate mental health care in underserved rural communities. We believe that where you live shouldn't determine whether you can access life-changing treatments like TMS therapy and Spravato.

Your donation is 100% tax-deductible and directly supports patients who cannot afford the full cost of treatment, community education programs, and expanding access to innovative mental health therapies in Millard County and beyond.
