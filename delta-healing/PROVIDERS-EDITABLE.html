About TMS Therapy

FDA-Cleared Treatment

NeuroStar TMS is FDA-cleared for treatment-resistant depression and backed by 20+ years of clinical research.

Mechanism of Action

Magnetic pulses stimulate underactive brain regions associated with mood regulation, particularly the left dorsolateral prefrontal cortex.

Treatment Protocol

Standard course: 36 sessions over 6-9 weeks, typically 5 days/week. Each session lasts 19-37 minutes depending on protocol.

Safety Profile

Well-tolerated with minimal side effects. Most common: mild headache or scalp discomfort during treatment, typically transient.

Ideal Candidates for TMS

TMS may be appropriate for patients who:

  • Have Major Depressive Disorder (MDD) or treatment-resistant depression
  • Have not achieved adequate symptom relief with antidepressant medications
  • Are unable to tolerate medication side effects
  • Are age 18 or older (some insurances cover 15+)
  • Do not have contraindications (ferromagnetic implants near head, active mania, seizure history)

Delta Healing Solutions completes formal medical appropriateness and insurance eligibility determinations for all referrals.

PHQ-9 Monitoring & Outcomes

We use the PHQ-9 as a standardized clinical instrument for screening, establishing baseline symptom burden, and monitoring treatment progress.

Response Definition

Patient achieves ≥50% reduction in baseline PHQ-9 score during treatment.

Remission Definition

Final PHQ-9 score of ≤4 at treatment completion.

After treatment completion, we send PHQ-9 follow-up surveys every 2-3 months to track durability and identify symptom recurrence early.
